Thrift Shop 🛍️

Shop for a cause — every purchase directly saves an animal's life.

Where Shopping Saves Lives

The Wags to Whiskers Thrift Shop opened in June 2024 in Kingwood, Texas — and every single dollar raised goes directly toward the care, medical vetting, and rescue of our animals. When you shop or donate items here, you're not just getting a great deal — you're giving a pet a second chance.

We accept donations of gently used clothing, housewares, furniture, books, toys, and more. Stop in, browse our ever-changing inventory, and take something home knowing it made a real difference.

Our Thrift Shop at 1417 Northpark Dr, Kingwood, TX — open Wednesday through Saturday.
