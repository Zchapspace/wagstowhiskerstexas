The Board

Meet the passionate volunteers who lead our rescue and make our mission possible.

Teri Kropik

Founder

Teri founded Wags to Whiskers of Texas with a heartfelt commitment to saving animals that would otherwise have no chance. Her passion and leadership built the foundation that our rescue stands on today.

Wanda Weisgerber

Board Member

Wanda brings professional pet-care expertise as the owner of Tail Waggers Pet Sitting. Her knowledge and dedication to animal welfare make her an invaluable member of our team.

Barbara Egan

Board Member

Barbara is a dedicated Vizsla lover and active member of the Fort Manahasset Cat Trunk & Animal Society (FMCTAS). She brings a wealth of community animal rescue experience to our board.
