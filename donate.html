Support Our Mission ♥

Every dollar goes directly to the animals — their medical care, food, and shelter.

Your Generosity Saves Lives

Wags to Whiskers of Texas is an all-volunteer, non-profit rescue. We receive no government funding — every rescue, every vet visit, every bag of food is made possible by generous donors like you. There are many easy ways to give:

💳

Donate via PayPal

Quick and secure — donate any amount directly through PayPal. One-time or recurring gifts welcome.

🛍️

Kroger Community Rewards

Link your Kroger Plus card to Wags to Whiskers and Kroger donates a portion of every purchase at no cost to you.

🎗️

GoFundMe

Support our ongoing campaigns on GoFundMe — great for sharing with friends and family.

✉️

Cash or Check

Prefer to give by check? Please make checks payable to Wags to Whiskers of Texas. Contact us for our mailing address.

🐱

Sponsor a Cat

Love a specific animal? Become a monthly sponsor and directly support one of our resident cats with food, medical care, and love.
