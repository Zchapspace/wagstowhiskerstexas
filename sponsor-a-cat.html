Sponsor a Cat 🐱

Can't adopt right now? Become a monthly sponsor and directly fund the care of a specific cat.

What is a Cat Sponsorship?

Some of our cats are long-term residents who need ongoing medical care or have special needs that make adoption more challenging. Your monthly sponsorship directly funds their food, veterinary care, and supplies — giving them a comfortable, loving life while they wait for the right forever family.

Sponsorships are available at three giving levels. Choose monthly or make an annual gift and save.
