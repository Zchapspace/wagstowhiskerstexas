Welcome to the Family!

Thank you for considering adoption through Wags to Whiskers of Texas. We are a non-profit, no-kill rescue dedicated to finding loving forever homes for cats, kittens, and other animals in need.

Before bringing your new companion home, please download and review the appropriate adoption contract. Completing paperwork in advance helps us process your adoption quickly so you can focus on what matters — giving your new pet all the love they deserve.

If you have any questions about the adoption process, please contact us or reach out at your local adoption event.
