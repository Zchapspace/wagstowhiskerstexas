Foster & Volunteer Programs

Join our community of dedicated volunteers and open your heart — and home — to animals in need.

Make a Real Difference

Wags to Whiskers of Texas runs entirely on the dedication of our volunteers. Whether you can open your home as a foster, help at weekend adoption events, or serve as an adoption counselor — every role directly saves animal lives.

We welcome volunteers of all ages (minors with parental permission). Download the appropriate forms below to get started, and contact us with any questions about our programs.

🏠

Foster Program

Foster families provide a temporary loving home for animals awaiting adoption. You provide the space and love — we provide the supplies, food, and veterinary care. Fostering is the heartbeat of our rescue.
