Spot Light ⭐

Share your rescued pet's story — they could be our next Pet of the Week!

Share Your Story

Did you adopt or rescue a pet through Wags to Whiskers? We'd love to hear all about your new family member! Submit a photo and a short story about your rescue pet, and they may be featured in our Pet of the Week Spot Light corner.

Every Spot Light story inspires others to adopt, foster, and support our mission. Your pet's story could be the reason someone else gives an animal their forever home.

📸

How to Submit

Send your pet's photo and a short description of their rescue story to us by email:

Submit Your Story →

Please include: your pet's name, a brief story (2–5 sentences), and one or more photos.
