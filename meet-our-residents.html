Meet Our Residents

Browse our adoptable animals and find your perfect companion waiting for a forever home.

Find Your New Best Friend

Our animals are in loving foster homes throughout the Houston area, receiving the best care while they wait for their forever families. Browse our listings on PetFinder to see who is currently available.

Once you find an animal you love, visit us at one of our Saturday adoption events or download adoption forms to get started.

Browse on PetFinder

View all our currently available animals on PetFinder — updated regularly with new arrivals.

How Adoption Works

1

Browse & Choose

Find an animal you love on PetFinder or at one of our Saturday events.

2

Download Forms

Download our adoption forms and review them before your visit.

3

Meet in Person

Visit us at a Saturday adoption event to meet your future companion.

4

Welcome Home!

Complete your paperwork, pay the adoption fee, and welcome your new best friend home.

Support Our Animals

Adoption fees help fund veterinary care, food, and supplies. You can also donate directly.
